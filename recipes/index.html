Chocolate baklava bracelets

Ingredients

For the syrup

  • 400 g granulated sugar
  • 400 g water
  • 1 stick(s) cinnamon
  • lemon peels, of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon(s) glucose

For the baklava bracelets

  • 450 g phyllo dough sheets
  • 100 g butter, melted

For the ganache

  • 150 g heavy cream 35%
  • 200 g chocolate couverture
  • 1 tablespoon(s) butter

To serve

  • pistachios, finely chopped

Preperation

For the syrup

  1. In a pot add the sugar, the water, the cinnamon, the lemon peels, the glucose, and stir.
  2. Transfer over medium heat and allow 2-3minutes for it to boil and for the sugar to melt.
  3. Remove and set aside to cool.

For the baklava bracelets

  1. Preheat the oven to 180ο C (350ο F) set to fan.
  2. Take one phyllo dough sheet and drizzle it with butter.
  3. Set a 40 cm steel skewer at the edge of the sheet’s shorter side and start rolling, leaving a 3-4 cm gap at the other side of the phyllo.
  4. Shirr the phyllo, remove the skewer, and curve the phyllo so that the two edges stick together and the unrolled phyllo forms a base at the center.
  5. Follow the same process for all of the phyllo dough sheets.
  6. Transfer the bracelets to a buttered 30x40 cm baking pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and with a serving spoon, pour over the cold syrup right away.
  8. Set aside for 3-4 hours until the whole syrup is absorbed.

For the chocolate ganache

  1. In a saucepan, add the heavy cream and transfer over medium heat. Let it come to a boil.
  2. Turn off the heat and add the chocolate couverture chopped into pieces.
  3. Wait a few seconds for the chocolate to start melting and then, stir with a spoon until the ingredients are homogenized.
  4. Add the butter and stir until it melts.
  5. Fill the center of the bracelets with the ganache, sprinkle with chopped pistachios, and serve.
